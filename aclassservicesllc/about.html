CLASS SERVICES LLC

Welcome to A Class Services LLC, your premier transportation provider in the Milwaukee area and beyond. Our team of experienced and highly trained drivers is committed to delivering top-notch transportation services to meet our clients' needs- individuals and groups. Our vision is to expand this nationwide as soon as possible. Having been in the industry for more than seven years, we have earned a reputation for delivering exceptional, dependable, efficient transportation services for a wide range of events.

Whether you need a ride to the airport, a corporate event, a wedding, or any other special occasion or need an airport shuttle service, we have you covered. We are much more than just a limousine business! Our fleet comprises a range of luxury vehicles, including limousines, sedans, SUVs, and minivans, designed to cater to different group sizes, from kids and youth to young adults and seniors and preferences. All our vehicles are well-maintained, registered by state, and insured to ensure your safety and comfort during your trip. At A Class Services LLC, we take pride in our professional transportation services and solutions. Our courteous customer service staff and professional drivers are committed to making your trip as smooth and comfortable as possible. We offer door-to-door service, and our drivers are always punctual and available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. With a professional chauffeur by your side, you'll arrive at your destination on time using the quickest and most suitable route. Plus, you'll have plenty of time to grab a bite, sip a drink, and stroll to your gate without feeling rushed. We offer flexible and customizable packages to fit your specific requirements. Our rates are competitive, and we offer transparent pricing with no hidden charges. Book with us for a comfortable and hassle-free ride! We look forward to crafting an unforgettable transportation experience.
